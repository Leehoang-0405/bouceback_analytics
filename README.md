## The Problem: The "Broken" Decision
The existing "Bounceback" campaign relied on a fixed multiplier model. Regardless of a donor's tenure or historical frequency, they received standardized "ask ladders."

**Structural Issues Identified:**
* **Over-asking:** Loyal donors faced fatigue and disengagement.
* **Under-asking:** High-capacity donors left potential revenue unrealized.
* **Uniformity Bias:** New and established donors were treated as behaviorally identical.

### **Business Impact**
* **Revenue Exposure:** Approximately **$775K** was at risk due to donor drop-off.
* **High Churn:** Consistent turnover exceeding 50% threatened the organization's sustainability.
* **Inefficiency:** Rising acquisition costs were required to replace lost supporters.
